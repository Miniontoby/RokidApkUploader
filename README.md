# RokidApkUploader

Just a simple Android app that uses the Rokid CXR-M SDK to upload/sideload an APK to your Rokid Glasses over Wi-Fi without dev cable.

This app also does NOT require a license file, just fill in your glasses serial number and all shall be fine.


## Download

You can download this MOBILE app at the https://github.com/Miniontoby/RokidApkUploader/releases
Download the .apk and install it

## Usage

The usage of this app can be very frustrating (I can tell you already: IT IS).
But its pretty straight forward never the less

1. Fold the right leg of your glasses
2. Press capture button 3 times very fast
3. Open your bluetooth settings
4. Open "Show All"
5. Disconnect your glasses
6. Now open the RokidApkUploader app
7. Fill in your glasses serial number (you have to, im sorry)
8. Click Select button and select an APK
9. Click Scan button and wait for it to find your glasses
10. Unfold the leg
11. Now click the Upload button
12. When prompted, click connect to glasses. But only accept it if it shows a PIN code, else click cancel!
13. When connection was successful, it will tell you in the glasses
14. After it told you, it start wifi and starts uploading. Status can be checked in the mobile app
15. After its done uploading it disconnects from wifi and disconnects from the glasses
